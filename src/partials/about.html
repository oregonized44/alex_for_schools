For Schools

Core Concepts

Being prepared for college and the workforce is important! I wan't to focus on promoting core skill development in the classroom to set students up to succeed moving forward.

Graduation Rates

I want to address graduation rates by promoting and sparking kids interest in college earlier on, specifially targeting middle school students - letting students know the possibilites the future has in store for them, as well, creating outlets and pathways for them to acheive those goals.

Inspire Learning

Collaborating with local companies and programs I aim to find new ways of sparking students interest in school by allowing for more opportunites for students to gain desired skills.
